What are we trying to do?

There's not one single source of anxiety for Americans - it's a complex mix of bills, unexpected incomes, wage theft, debt, etc. That's why the path to financial security is not a single step - rather a series to first protect, then stabilize, and then finally to improve American's financial health.


The following are issues we want to explore in each category.


Stay Afloat

  • Making it to Payday
    How can we make sure you can pay without worrying about payday?
  • Handling Unexpected Expenses
    How can we make sure you can pay for now without risking the future?

Be Safe & Secure

  • Managing Debt & Bills
    How can we make sure you can manage and understand your bills?
  • Getting Paid Properly
    How can we make sure you are being properly and on-time?

Grow Confidence

  • Understanding How & Why To Save
    How can we make sure we get you to save?
  • Finding the Best Loans and Debt
    How can we make sure you're getting the best deals?
  • Making Goals
    How can we make sure we're making goals to improve your situation?
