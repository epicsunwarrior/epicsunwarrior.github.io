Direct Financial Assistance

Find Some Space to Breathe

In life, things go sideways - and when it does, it’ll probably cost you. Escape the stress of living paycheck to paycheck with 125th as your safety net.

Stay on Top of Your Debt and Expenses

Ignorance in sometimes bliss, but unfortunately bliss leads to more stress later. Let’s find a way to regain control of your bills and debt with the assist from 125th through guidance and direct assistance.


Short Term Planning

Develop a Budget that Works for You

Everyone recommends making a budget, but lets get you a budget that aligns with your day-to-day. 125th can help smooth out the gaps, and figure out what to pay and when to pay.

Get Freedom and Flexibility

Peace of mind comes from not worrying about finances all the time. Use 125th to help you mind the details and develop a strategy to remove stress.


Long Term Guidance

Make Decisions with Confidence

Not all of us are lucky enough to have our parents teach us the ropes. Confidence comes from an understanding and knowledge of your finances. Let’s give you a playbook to connect those dots towards a better future.

Break the Cycle

Financial peace of mind is possible. Lean on 125th as you build your rainy day fund; we believe you are capable of all that and more.
