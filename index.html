We Need Some Advice!

We're looking to make New Yorkers less stressed and anxious about their finances. And we've done the paper research, but we need to talk to more New Yorkers on how they manage their finances and their financial anxiety.


What are we trying to do?

There's not one single source of anxiety for Americans - it's a storm of unexpected expenses, income or expense flucations, burden of debt, etc. And that there's not a single golden bullet - rather than a series of steps to first protect then to improve.
